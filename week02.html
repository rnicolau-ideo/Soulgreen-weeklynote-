Week 02
Fresh from the field.

Welcome to the second Project Alchemist weekly note, your regular window into the work as it happens. Week two is done and what a week it was.

Take a look at what we got up to below, and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions.

What we did
  • We explored analogous inspiration beyond the supplement market to expand our perspective and gather fresh ideas. This took us to the gut health app ZOE, AI companions like Tolen and Replika, competitors like Biogena, and innovative concepts like The Ordinary, SoulCycle, Nespresso, and the Starbucks flagship store in Milan. We also took advantage of Design Week and popped into the IKEA exhibition centred around social habits and food. We'll share our learnings in more detail next week.
  • We interviewed six participants in their homes across Milan, learning about their habits around health, AI, and supplements. These were rich, honest conversations that gave us a much clearer picture of your potential consumer: their habits, challenges, and preferences.
  • We gathered video input from five additional participants. While less in-depth than the home interviews, they added a valuable layer of insight to what we're building.
  • To complement our research, we spoke to two experts: a personal trainer who specialises in neurodivergent clients, and a sociologist who specialises in the relationship between humans and AI.
  • We have now downloaded everything we've collected and are laser focused on preparing for our storytelling session, where we'll walk through the most interesting threads from the research and make sure the whole team has a comprehensive view of everything gathered before we move into synthesis. It's going to be an energising session and we're so glad you'll be joining us.
