Week 03
Into the insights.

As we close out week three, this has been one of those weeks where everything starts to click. We moved from raw research into crystallised insights, giving us the foundation we need to start designing.

As always, have a read below and feel free to reach out with any questions.

What we did
  • The team worked through a rigorous synthesis of all the field research from Milan, pulling threads across interviews and observations to distil core insights about how people really relate to supplements, trust, and AI in their daily lives. Each insight comes paired with a "what does this mean for Soulgreen?" question to start opening up possibilities.
  • Earlier in the week we ran a multi-part storytelling session with the Soulgreen team. Over several hours, we walked through interview highlights in a structured playback format, bringing customer voices to life. We also shared our analogous inspiration and expert interview findings, giving the team direct access to perspectives from specialists in nutrition, behaviour change, and digital health. Thank you to everyone who participated. Your energy and input made our day!
  • On Thursday, we presented our research insights to the full Soulgreen team in a structured two-hour session, covering research grounding and a deep insight walkthrough.
  • With everyone aligned on the outcomes of our research, we moved into a collaborative making session to begin shaping early vision directions. We built a custom interactive tool to support real-time collaboration and get everyone contributing their ideas and preliminary vision statements. This is where research moves into design.
