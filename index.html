Week 01
And we're live.

Welcome to the first Project Alchemist weekly note, a way to keep you close to the work as it happens. Here's where we are, what we've been building, and what's coming next.

Why 'Project Alchemist'? Because supplementation is, at its core, about transformation. Every touchpoint, every interaction, every detail of the experience should feel like it's facilitating that change. Alchemist felt like a good shorthand to keep us true to that idea as we move forward.

Take a look at what we've been up to below and feel free to reply with any questions!

What we did
  • We opened the project with a session with all of you: walking through the plan and targets, and exploring your hypotheses, hopes, and fears for the road ahead. Thank you all for the energy you brought to that session!
  • We sat down with the Soulgreen team to learn about the origins of the brand and the vision behind it.
  • We dug into the documents you shared and absorbed the work already done by Zelo, Interbrand, and BIP.
  • We had an onboarding call with BIP to sync on coordination and next steps.
  • We handled all the logistics to kick off our research stage next week: recruiting participants and developing the discussion guides and stimuli we'll use during interviews.
  • We started our analogous research, which means looking outside the immediate sector to bring fresh perspective into the work. The team is exploring apps like ZOE, Replika, and Tolan to learn from memberships that include an AI companion. We also visited a Biogena shop to experience their personalised skin scan, dropped into The Ordinary to explore their simplified, science-led approach to skincare, and attended a SoulCycle class to see how a well-designed experience can build a global community.
NEXT WEEK

Next week we head into the field: meeting participants in their homes across Milan and the surrounding area, conducting interviews, continuing our analogous research, and seeing you in person. We can't wait.
