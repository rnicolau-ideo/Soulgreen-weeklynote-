This week
And we're live.

Welcome to the first Project Alchemist weekly note: a way to keep you close to the work. Here's where we are, what we've been building, and what's coming next. As always, feel free to reply with any questions.

We named it Project Alchemist because we wanted to capture two things at once: the scientific rigour behind Soulgreen's product, and the sense of magic we want to infuse into the experience. Alchemist felt like the right word for that combination. It seemed like a good place to start.

What we did

We kicked off.
We opened the project with a session with all of you: walking through the plan and targets, and exploring your hypotheses, hopes, and fears for the road ahead.

We went to the source.
We sat down with the Soulgreen team to learn about the origins of the brand and the individual vision behind it.

We did our homework.
We dug into the documents you shared and absorbed the work already done by Zelo, Interbrand, and BIP: building on the foundation that's already there.

We got aligned.
We had an onboarding call with BIP to sync on deadlines and coordination.

We prepared for Italy.
We handled all the logistics to kick off our research stage next week: recruiting participants and developing the discussion guides and stimuli we'll use during interviews.

We looked sideways.
We started our analogous research: looking outside the immediate sector to bring fresh perspective into the work. The team is exploring apps like ZOE, Replika, and Tolen to understand what membership to an AI companion might feel like. We also visited a Biogena shop to experience their personalised skin scan and supplements offering, and attended a SoulCycle class to see how a specific experience can build community.

NEXT WEEK

Next week we go where the real insights live: meeting participants in their homes across Milan and the surrounding area, conducting interviews, continuing our analogous research, and meeting you face to face.
